dm2 : Diallel Method 2 analysis for RCBD and Alpha Lattice

Nandan Patil

Function dm2 conducts Diallel Method 2 analysis for RCBD and Alpha Lattice design.

Example 1: Diallel Method 2 analysis for RCBD design.
